The Walking Dead

The Walking Dead is a post-apocalyptic horror drama about a group of survivors fighting to stay alive amid a zombie outbreak and dangerous human factions. Based on the comic series by Robert Kirkman and others, it launched the wider franchise and follows shifting alliances, moral struggles, and constant threats from both walkers and people.

Seasons 1 Compilation

Season 1 of The Walking Dead aired on AMC from October 31 to December 5, 2010, with 6 episodes. Developed by Frank Darabont—who wrote most episodes and directed the pilot—it adapts the comic series by Robert Kirkman, Tony Moore, and Charlie Adlard and was produced by Darabont, Kirkman, and others.

Season 2 Compilation

Season 2 of The Walking Dead aired from October 16, 2011, to March 18, 2012, with 13 episodes. Based on the comic series, it was produced by Frank Darabont, Robert Kirkman, and others, with Glen Mazzara taking over as showrunner after Darabont left.

Season 3

Season 3 of The Walking Dead aired from October 14, 2012, to March 31, 2013, with 16 episodes. Produced by Robert Kirkman, Glen Mazzara, and others, it marked Mazzara’s final season as showrunner and earned strong critical acclaim, winning two Saturn Awards.

Season 4

Season 4 of The Walking Dead ran from October 13, 2013, to March 30, 2014, with 16 episodes. Scott M. Gimple took over as showrunner, and the season earned strong reviews, winning three awards including Best Syndicated/Cable Television Series at the 40th Saturn Awards.

Season 5

Season 5 of The Walking Dead aired from October 12, 2014, to March 29, 2015, with 16 episodes. Led by showrunner Scott M. Gimple, it earned major acclaim and won three awards, including a third straight Saturn Award for Best Syndicated/Cable Television Series.

Season 6

Season 6 of The Walking Dead aired from October 11, 2015, to April 3, 2016, with 16 episodes. Scott M. Gimple returned as showrunner, and the season earned strong reviews and three awards, including Best Horror Television Series at the 42nd Saturn Awards.

Season 7

Season 7 of The Walking Dead aired from October 23, 2016, to April 2, 2017, with 16 episodes. With Scott M. Gimple still as showrunner, it drew mixed reviews but won three awards, including a second straight Saturn Award for Best Horror Television Series.

Season 8

Season 8 of The Walking Dead aired from October 22, 2017, to April 15, 2018, with 16 episodes. Scott M. Gimple’s final season as showrunner earned mixed reviews but won two awards, including a third consecutive Saturn Award for Best Horror Television Series.

Season 9

Season 9 of The Walking Dead aired from October 7, 2018, to March 31, 2019, with 16 episodes. Angela Kang became showrunner after Scott M. Gimple’s promotion, marking a new creative direction for the series.

Season 10

Season 10 of The Walking Dead aired from October 6, 2019, to April 4, 2021, with 22 episodes. Angela Kang returned as showrunner, and the season received positive reviews, earning multiple award nominations, including a fifth consecutive Saturn Award for Best Horror Television Series.

Season 11

The eleventh and final season of The Walking Dead aired from August 22, 2021, to November 20, 2022, with 24 episodes. Angela Kang served her final season as showrunner, and the season received positive reviews.
