Discover Luisiana Laguna

Luisiana, officially the Municipality of Luisiana(Tagalog: Bayan ng Luisiana), is a municipality in the province of Laguna, Philippines.

Packages

Luisiana, Laguna offers several tour packages, primarily focusing on day hikes to its famous waterfalls, such as Hulugan Falls, Talay Falls, and Hidden Falls.
